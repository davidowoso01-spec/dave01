Our Academic Programmes

The Department offers academic programmes at Bachelor, Master's, and Ph.D. levels in various specializations in Cyber Security Science.

Undergraduate

B.Sc. Cyber Security Science

4 Years

Comprehensive bachelor degree providing foundational knowledge in cyber security principles and technologies.

Key Areas:
  • Network Security
  • Ethical Hacking
  • Digital Forensics
  • Cryptography
Postgraduate

PGD Cyber Security

1-2 Years

A specialized diploma for professionals seeking to advance their practical knowledge in cyber security.

Key Areas:
  • Risk Management
  • Security Architecture
  • Incident Response
  • Compliance
Master's Degree

M.Sc. Cyber Security

2 Years

Master degree programme with specializations in various domains of cyber security and information systems.

Key Areas:
  • Cloud Security
  • IoT Security
  • Advanced Cryptography
  • Thesis Work
Doctoral Degree

Ph.D. Cyber Security

3-5 Years

Doctoral programme for advanced research in cyber security, biometrics, and AI security.

Key Areas:
  • Original Research
  • Biometrics Security
  • AI in Cyber Security
  • Academic Publication
