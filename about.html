About The Department

Pioneering Excellence in Cyber Security Education and Research since 2019.

Our Story

The Department of Cyber Security Science at Ladoke Akintola University of Technology (LAUTECH) was established to address the growing global demand for digital security experts. As a cornerstone of the Faculty of Computing and Informatics, we have quickly become a premier destination for students seeking to defend the digital frontier.

Our curriculum is designed to bridge the gap between theoretical computer science and practical, real-world security applications, ensuring our graduates are "battle-ready" for the industry.

Our Mission

To produce high-quality graduates equipped with advanced skills in cyber defense, digital forensics, and ethical hacking to protect global digital infrastructure.

Our Vision

To be a world-class center of excellence in cyber security research and innovation, fostering a safer digital world through education and technology.

Core Values

Integrity

Upholding the highest ethical standards in all security practices.

Innovation

Staying ahead of cyber threats through constant research and new ideas.

Collaboration

Working with industry leaders to provide hands-on experience for students.
